Why Did We Choose to Make This Website?

We were tasked to make our first website for our Computer Science class, and we chose to make a website about ourselves and our classmates since we wanted our first website to be as special as our section. This showcases our talents, skills, and can further be a source of pride for our section. We also wanted to make a website that would be useful for our classmates and future students. Overall, this website stands as a representation of our section and what we have been through together not just as a class—but as a family.

What Is the Purpose of This Website?

The main purpose of this website is to showcase the talents and skills of our section, Beryllium. It also serves as a platform for us to share our stories and experiences. Additionally, this website can be a useful resource for future students who want to learn more about our section and the people in it. Even after this school year ends, this website will still be accessible to anyone who wants to visit it and be a reminder of our time together as a section. In the end, this website is a testament to our section's unity, creativity, and resilience.
