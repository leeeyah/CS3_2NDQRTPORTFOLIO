Meet our Adviser

Our class adviser is Ma'am Marivice Joyce Villa-Ong, a one of a kind, caring, responsible, and understanding teacher. Beyond being our advisers, she is also our teacher for Physics 1, one of the hardest subjects in our current grade level. However, she stays determined in helping us through our dilemas and problems, always offering a helping hand and giving us motivating advice every homeroom. She never forgets to ensure that we are doing well, going as far as to weekly checking up on each classmate and offering to listen to those who want to share their hardships with her.

A Glimpse of Our Section

Beryllium is a one of the sections of the 9th Grade in PSHS Main Campus. It is composed of 30 students, with its class president being Rafaela Yatco. Beryllium is an energetic, cooperative, and optimistic section, always ready to lift each other up and stand up for each other. We are a hardworking, competitive, and determined section, always striving to be the best that we can be. Moreover, we make sure to always have fun, enjoy high school life as a class, and make unforgettable memories together. Beryllium 2029 is not just a section, but truly a family.
